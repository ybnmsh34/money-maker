What is URL Encoding?

URL encoding (percent-encoding) converts characters into a format that can be transmitted over the Internet. Characters that have special meaning in URLs (like spaces, &, =) are replaced with a % followed by two hexadecimal digits.

Encode uses encodeURI() — encodes a full URL, keeping protocol and domain intact. Encode Component uses encodeURIComponent() — encodes everything, useful for URL parameters.
